Your Voice is the Most Important Part of Your Brand

It’s easy to feel overwhelmed by social media. It seems like there’s a new tool or trick to learn about every day. By the time you’ve adjusted to one Facebook layout, it changes again. How do you stay ahead of the next big thing? What should you focus on?

We’re here to tell you there’s one thing that will always matter, no matter what comes next: your voice.

Your voice — what you talk about and how you say it — is the most important part of your brand on social media. Your voice communicates value. It informs your audience. It’s how your audience recognizes you. It doesn’t matter if you’re on Twitter, Facebook, your blog, or the next big thing: your audience will know you by your voice.

We built Measured Voice to help you work with your team to develop a strong and clear brand voice. Here’s how:

  • Scheduled messages let you get your messages out when and how frequently you want  to. Want to be a chatterbox? That’s fine. Want to say only the right things at the perfect times? That’s fine too. Scheduled messages let you speak strategically, when it makes sense for you and your audience.
  • Guidelines are built right in to Measured Voice. We’ve found that a simple list of reminders of what to talk about, who your audience is, and what your goals are is a powerful way to  keep your voice on brand.
  • Post-by-post metrics let you see which messages work and which ones fall flat. Over time, you’ll learn how to speak your audience’s language.
  • Unlimited users make it easy to bring in ideas and editing from your entire team. Your brand’s voice should reflect the people behind it.
  • An easy workflow allows editors to ensure that all messages written in Measured Voice are on voice before they go out.

Taking the time and attention to develop a strong brand voice ensures that you will be ready for whatever comes next in social media. We’re here to help you do it.
